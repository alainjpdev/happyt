Our heart

We believe that every child is exceptional, and we want to provide each one with the appropriate context to find their calling. In a future that moves at the speed of innovation, difficult to predict; our mission is to prepare children to be happy, kind, and productive members of a global community.

How we do it

More than a method, it is a philosophy with the method that each child needs. We do this with the best talent, creating a community of awakened families that understand the need for new educational models.
We recognize that each child is different, and that's why we use the method and pace that each one needs in their learning and growth process (hyper-personalized method).
We teach inside and outside the classrooms, based on projects that cut across school subjects and life skills.
We also use Yoshindo as a non-violent sport and martial art, which reinforces the educational philosophy with skills such as self-control, positive discipline, concentration, and respect, building self-esteem and self-confidence.

Impact

Social impact: Due to our level of specialization, personalized focus and inclusion of neuro-diverse children, Happy Tribe is committed to creating employment with above-market compensation. To date, we have positively impacted the lives of more than 100 children who either did not fit into the school system, were unhappy or didn't progress at their own pace with traditional methods.
We have discovered genius children who were previously labeled as "problems" through knowledge, as well as an appropriate and personalized method. With this project, we hope to positively influence thousands of children. This not only impacts the children and their families but also the community, with happy, productive children who are kind members of a multicultural community.
Neurodiversity inclusion - Happy Tribe is the only educational and developmental offering for neurodiverse children in Puerto Aventuras (Autism, ADHD, others). Currently, Happy Tribe is the reason why some families decide to move from other places to Puerto Aventuras.

Our story

Happy Tribe Puerto Aventuras was born 100% from the heart, with Daniela homeschooling her daughter (due to a lack of innovative schools for her), incorporating specialized learning in diverse educational methods, sports, martial arts, and even nutrition to achieve the best result aligned with what she needed. Family and friends quickly noticed the results of the educational philosophy, the heart, and affection in the process, so Dani accepted 8 more students into her home in 2021. The passion, affection, and effectiveness of the method led to 38 students joining in the first year through word of mouth. It was not Dani seeking more students, but the same families and the community asking her to accept more of their children. Until today, Happy Tribe has grown to 70 students, 100% driven by the community.
Understanding the value of what is being done and the opportunity to do more and better; in 2023, Dani, David, and Jorge combined passion, knowledge, and the same calling to serve children and youth, to formally found Happy Tribe as a project aimed at reaching its maximum potential in service of the families in the community. As founders, they are united by a passion for education and the future that awaits our children, and together they have over 30 years of experience in disruptive education, business, and innovation.
